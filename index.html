How entry works

We cap every intake at 20.

Not a growth hack — a moderation limit. Twenty is what we can onboard without the rooms getting noisy.

01

Apply

Two minutes. Your city, your device, what you're actually trying to fix. We read every one.

02

Get your queue number

You'll see your position in the current intake immediately. Reviews happen weekly.

03

Approval on WhatsApp

If you're in, we message you personally with your entry link. No bots, no funnel emails.

04

24 hours to confirm

Your seat is held for a day. Miss the window and it goes to the next person — you rejoin the queue for the next intake.

The 24-hour window exists because held seats block real applicants. If it lapses, no hard feelings — reapply, you just go back in line.
